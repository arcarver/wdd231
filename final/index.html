What are Dad Jokes?

Dad Jokes are jokes that are often told by fathers to their childern. These jokes often get groans or an eye roll from children and adults alike. They are usually clean jokes, so they are appropriate for all ages.

These jokes often include puns. Some dads have many dad jokes, while others have one or two jokes ready to go.

Non dads can also say dad jokes.
